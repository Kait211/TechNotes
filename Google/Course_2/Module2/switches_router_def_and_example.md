# Routers vs. Switches

## Switch
- Connects **devices on the same network**.
- Sends data to the **correct device**.

**Example:**
Your PC sends a file to your printer. The **switch** makes sure the file goes only to the printer.

> **Easy to remember:** Switch = **Device → Device**

---

## Router
- Connects **different networks**.
- Sends data between your **home network** and the **Internet**.

**Example:**
You open YouTube. The **router** sends your request from your home network to Google's network on the Internet.

> **Easy to remember:** Router = **Network → Network**

---

## House Analogy

- **Switch** = The hallway inside your house that helps you get to the right room.
- **Router** = The front door that connects your house to the outside world.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Switch | Router |
|--------|--------|
| Connects devices on the same network | Connects different networks |
| Sends data to the correct device | Sends data to the correct network |
| Used inside a home or office network | Connects your network to the Internet |

---

## One-Sentence Summary

- **Switch → Device → Device**
- **Router → Network → Network**

# Hub

## What is a Hub?
A **hub** is a basic networking device that connects **multiple devices on the same network**.

Unlike a switch, a hub **doesn't know where data should go**. It sends the data to **every connected device**.

**Example:**
Your PC sends a file to your printer. The **hub** sends that file to **every device** on the network. Only the printer uses it—the other devices ignore it.

> **Easy to remember:** Hub = **Everyone gets the data**

---

## House Analogy

- **Hub** = Someone in your house who **shouts a message to everyone**, even if only one person needed to hear it.
- **Switch** = Someone who **walks directly to the correct person** and tells only them.
- **Router** = The **front door** that connects your house to the outside world.

---

## Hub vs. Switch vs. Router

| Device | What it does | Easy to Remember |
|---------|--------------|------------------|
| **Hub** | Sends data to **every** device | Everyone gets it |
| **Switch** | Sends data to the **correct** device | Device → Device |
| **Router** | Connects **different networks** | Network → Network |

---

## One-Sentence Summary

- **Hub → Everyone**
- **Switch → One Device**
- **Router → Another Network**
